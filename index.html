Frequently asked

Do I need to already own a mail center?+

No. We work with owners at every stage — from picking a location and negotiating a lease, to running an established center that's plateaued.

What if I already know PostalMate pretty well?+

Most owners know the software's basics but aren't using it to make pricing and staffing decisions. The audit is about turning your existing data into decisions, not teaching you buttons.

How is this different from just calling PostalMate support?+

Support can tell you how a feature works. We tell you whether your pricing, staffing, and layout are actually costing you money — and what to change first.

Can we start with a single call before committing to mentorship?+

Yes — the strategy call is the starting point for every engagement, no packages required upfront.
